About Us

Our Mission

Inspiring Success in Education

At Leading Tuition, our mission is to foster an environment where students are encouraged to flourish. We strive to provide top-quality tutoring, personalized learning experiences, and ongoing support to help every student achieve their academic goals.

We believe that every student has the potential to succeed when given the right guidance, encouragement, and resources. Our approach combines expert teaching with a genuine commitment to building confidence and independence in learning.

With more than 7,000 hours of lessons delivered and close to 500 students taught, we have built a strong community founded on trust, dedication, and proven results.

Alexander Meister

Business and Operations Manager

With over a decade of experience in education, Alex has taught students worldwide, and guided tutors, schools and families in adopting effective learning strategies. His unique background, spanning both teaching and business development, allows him to combine academic expertise with the operational excellence required to deliver results.

Arun Sharma

Founder and Head of Learning

Leading Tuition was founded by Arun, who studied Engineering at the University of Oxford. During his time there, he discovered a passion for tutoring and saw first-hand how the right support could transform a student's confidence and achievement.

Driven by this experience, Arun set out to make top-quality education accessible to more families. His vision and dedication have shaped Leading Tuition into what it is today: a trusted partner known for excellence, care, and results.

Under his leadership, the company has guided hundreds of students through GCSEs, A-Levels, Oxbridge preparation, and university admissions, helping them not only reach but exceed their goals.
