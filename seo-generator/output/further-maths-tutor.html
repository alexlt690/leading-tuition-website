## Further Maths Tutoring Further Mathematics is one of the most demanding A-Level subjects, covering advanced topics such as complex numbers, matrices, further calculus, and mechanics that go well beyond the standard Maths syllabus. Our specialist Further Maths tutors work with students across all major exam boards — including Edexcel, AQA, and OCR — helping them build the deep conceptual understanding and problem-solving confidence that top universities expect. Whether your child is struggling to keep pace with a fast-moving school curriculum, preparing for challenging entrance exams like STEP or MAT, or simply aiming to secure the highest grades, we match them with an experienced tutor who can tailor every session to their specific needs and learning style. Sessions can take place online or in person, fitting around busy school timetables, and parents receive regular progress updates so you always know how your child is developing.
