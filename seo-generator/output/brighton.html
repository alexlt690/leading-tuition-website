Private Tuition in Brighton

Expert tutors supporting families across Brighton

Brighton is one of the most educationally ambitious cities on the south coast, and the pressure parents feel here reflects that. Whether your child is preparing for entry to Brighton College or Brighton and Hove High School, working towards A-Levels at BHASVIC or Varndean Sixth Form College, or targeting a place at a Russell Group university, the academic stakes feel very real. At Leading Tuition, we work with Brighton families across all of these pathways, providing structured, expert support that is tailored to your child's school, their exam board, and their individual goals.

Tutoring in Brighton — What We Offer

Leading Tuition provides one-to-one private tuition for students from primary age through to university entry. Our tutors are subject specialists, many of whom have direct experience with the schools and sixth forms your child attends or is applying to. We work with students across Brighton and the surrounding areas, including Hove, Kemptown, Fiveways, Preston Park, and further afield into East Sussex towards Lewes.

Sessions are delivered online, giving families the flexibility to fit tuition around school commitments, extracurricular activities, and the realities of a busy household. Every student is matched with a tutor whose subject knowledge and teaching style suits them — not simply whoever is available.

GCSE and A-Level Support

The majority of secondary schools in Brighton and Hove follow OCR and Edexcel specifications, and our tutors are thoroughly familiar with both. This matters more than it might seem. A tutor who knows the precise structure of an Edexcel GCSE Mathematics paper, or the specific essay conventions expected in an OCR A-Level History mark scheme, can make a meaningful difference to how a student prepares and performs.

At GCSE level, we support students across core subjects including Mathematics, English Language and Literature, the Sciences, History, Geography, and Modern Foreign Languages. We pay close attention to the assessment objectives that examiners use, helping students understand not just the content but how to demonstrate their knowledge effectively under exam conditions.

At A-Level, demand for tuition is particularly strong among students at BHASVIC and Varndean Sixth Form College who are targeting competitive university courses. We regularly support students studying A-Level Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Further Mathematics, Economics, Psychology, and English Literature. For students at Dorothy Stringer School moving into sixth form, we also provide bridging support to help them transition confidently from GCSE to A-Level study.

Our tutors understand the specific demands of Edexcel A-Level Sciences and OCR Mathematics, and they structure sessions to build both conceptual understanding and exam technique in parallel.

11+ and Grammar School Preparation

Brighton and Hove does not have grammar schools, so the 11+ examination is not a feature of the local state school landscape in the way it is in parts of Kent or Buckinghamshire. However, a number of Brighton families do consider selective independent schools, where entrance assessments test reasoning, English, and Mathematics at a level that benefits from focused preparation.

For younger students, Leading Tuition offers structured support at primary and early secondary level, helping children build strong foundations in core subjects before the pressures of Key Stage 3 and GCSE begin. This kind of early investment in mathematical fluency, reading comprehension, and written communication pays dividends throughout a child's school career.

Medicine and University Admissions Preparation

Brighton has a growing number of students each year applying to study Medicine, Dentistry, and other highly competitive courses at leading universities. The admissions process for these courses is demanding, and preparation needs to begin well before the UCAS deadline.

Leading Tuition supports students with UCAT preparation, personal statement guidance, and interview coaching for medical school applications. Our tutors include those with direct experience of the admissions process at Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial, and other Russell Group institutions. We also support students applying for competitive non-medical courses — Law, Economics, Engineering, and Natural Sciences among them — where strong A-Level grades alone are rarely sufficient.

For students at Brighton College preparing for Oxbridge applications, we offer targeted support that addresses the specific demands of admissions tests and interview formats used by individual colleges and departments.

Frequently Asked Questions about Tutoring in Brighton

My child is at BHASVIC and struggling with their A-Level choices — can you help even mid-year?

Yes. We regularly take on students at any point in the academic year, including those who feel they have fallen behind after the first term. An early assessment session helps us understand exactly where the gaps are and build a plan that is realistic given the time available before exams.

Brighton and Hove doesn't have grammar schools — does that mean you don't offer any support for primary-age children?

Not at all. We work with primary and lower secondary students on building strong subject foundations, supporting children who need extra challenge as well as those who need consolidation. Strong early skills in Mathematics and English make a significant difference when GCSE preparation begins.

My child is applying to study Medicine and is sitting the UCAT — do your tutors have experience with this?

Yes. We support students through UCAT preparation with tutors who understand the structure and timing demands of each subtest. We also offer personal statement support and mock interview practice for medical school applications.

We live in Hove rather than Brighton itself — do you cover our area?

All of our tuition is delivered online, so your location within Brighton and Hove makes no difference to the quality or availability of support. Students in Hove, Kemptown, Preston Park, and across the wider area access exactly the same service.
