FAQs

At Leading Tuition, we provide expert academic support across every stage of a student's journey. Our tutoring covers all subjects at GCSE and A-Level, as well as later Pre-GCSE years to ensure students build a strong foundation before entering exam courses.

We also specialise in selective school entrance preparation, including the 11+ and 13+ exams, guiding students through the skills and strategies needed to succeed in highly competitive assessments. For those preparing to take the next step into higher education, we offer tailored support for university admissions tests such as the UCAT and other entry requirements.

In addition, our Oxbridge specialists provide one-to-one coaching for Oxbridge interviews, as well as personal statement guidance, ensuring that students not only meet but stand out in the demanding admissions process.

Whether the goal is to secure a selective school place, achieve top exam grades, or gain entry into a world-class university, Leading Tuition delivers the expertise, structure, and personal support needed to succeed.

1-on-1 lessons are completely flexible, parents and students choose the times that work best for them and when to take a break.

We don't run our GCSE Maths Bootcamps during school holidays, but we do occasionally host specialised holiday workshops designed to give students an extra boost in key areas. This balance allows families to maintain consistency in learning while still enjoying the flexibility of the holiday period.

We go beyond academic excellence to ensure the safety and wellbeing of every student. Alongside our rigorous hiring process, where we select only the most qualified and experienced tutors, we also require all tutors to hold an enhanced DBS check, which we keep on record.

Safeguarding is a responsibility we take very seriously. Parents can be confident that every tutor not only meets our high academic standards but also upholds the trust and care essential for a safe learning environment.
