**English Language Tutoring** Whether your child is preparing for GCSE English Language or simply needs support building stronger reading and writing skills, our experienced tutors provide focused, one-to-one sessions tailored to their individual needs. We cover all the key areas of the course, including analysing unseen fiction and non-fiction texts, crafting compelling creative and transactional writing, and developing the technical accuracy that examiners reward. Our tutors are familiar with the major exam board specifications — including AQA, Edexcel, and OCR — so every session is directly relevant to what your child will face in the classroom and in their exams. As well as boosting grades, we aim to build genuine confidence, helping students approach longer reading and writing tasks without anxiety. Sessions can be delivered online or in person, and we work flexibly around school timetables to make support as straightforward as possible for your family.
