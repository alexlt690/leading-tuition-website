Norwich has one of the most academically distinctive educational landscapes in the East of England. Norwich School — situated in the Cathedral Close — is one of the region's most respected independent day schools, and the University of East Anglia brings a creative and scholarly energy to the city that shapes local academic aspirations. Families across Eaton, Thorpe St Andrew, and Hellesdon are preparing children for competitive independent school entry, strong GCSE and A-Level outcomes, and university applications to institutions well beyond the region. At Leading Tuition, we support Norwich families with expert, targeted tuition at every stage.

Tutoring in Norwich — What We Offer

Leading Tuition provides one-to-one tuition from primary through to sixth form and university admissions. Our tutors are subject specialists matched to your child's exact school, syllabus, and target outcomes. Whether your child attends Norwich School, Notre Dame High School, City of Norwich School, Langley School, or another local school, we find the right tutor for their needs.

Sessions are available online and in person across Norwich, Eaton, Thorpe St Andrew, Sprowston, Costessey, Hellesdon, and the wider Norfolk area.

  • GCSE and A-Level support across all subjects
  • Tutors experienced with AQA and OCR specifications
  • Independent school support for Norwich School and Langley School
  • Medicine and university admissions preparation including UCAT
  • Flexible online and in-person sessions

GCSE and A-Level Support

Norfolk schools follow a mix of AQA and OCR specifications, and our tutors are matched to students based on their exact board and course. The step up from GCSE to A-Level is significant in all subjects, and students who receive targeted, board-specific support — rather than general revision — perform more consistently in their examinations.

Students at Norwich School are working within a demanding independent environment where the expectation is strong university outcomes, and many sixth-formers here are targeting Russell Group and competitive London universities. We support students across all A-Level subjects, with particular demand in the sciences, mathematics, English literature, and history.

For students at City of Norwich School or Notre Dame High School who are aiming high, one-to-one tuition provides the individual attention that allows them to reach the top of their ability range — which is what competitive university applications require.

11+ and Independent School Preparation

Norfolk has no grammar schools, so secondary transfer here does not involve the standard 11+ assessment. However, families considering Norwich School or Langley School (near Loddon, serving families across Norfolk) face competitive entry processes with their own assessments in English and Mathematics.

We support children from Year 3 upwards with the foundational skills that underpin performance in independent school entrance assessments. For families in Norwich considering boarding school options outside the county, we also support preparation for Common Entrance and other assessment formats used by schools elsewhere in the East of England and beyond.

Medicine and University Admissions Preparation

A number of Norwich's most able sixth-formers set their sights each year on medicine, dentistry, or other highly competitive degree programmes. Leading Tuition supports these students with UCAT preparation — which is now used by the majority of UK medical schools — personal statement development, and MMI interview coaching. We also support students targeting Oxbridge or competitive London universities with admissions test preparation and interview coaching.

Why Norwich Families Choose Leading Tuition

We understand what Norwich families are working towards. The city's educational landscape combines strong independent school aspiration with the quietly high expectations that a university town generates. Our tutors bring genuine subject expertise and an understanding of the specific schools and examination boards that Norwich students encounter — and we communicate clearly with parents throughout.

Frequently Asked Questions about Tutoring in Norwich

Does Norwich School have a competitive entrance process?

Yes. Norwich School is academically selective and sets its own entrance assessments in English and Mathematics, with the standard reflecting its strong university outcomes. Children who perform well have typically had structured preparation — not just good general ability — and approach the assessment with confidence in the format and the content it tests.

My daughter is at City of Norwich School and aiming for a Russell Group university — can you help?

Yes. A-Level subject choice, examination technique, personal statement support, and targeted tuition in the subjects where she is working hardest are all areas we support. Students from state schools who are targeting competitive universities benefit particularly from one-to-one support that is calibrated to the specific course content and examination board they are sitting.

We are in Thorpe St Andrew — do you cover our area?

Yes. We work with families across Norwich and the wider Norfolk area including Thorpe St Andrew, Eaton, Hellesdon, Sprowston, and Costessey. Most sessions are conducted online, but in-person is available where preferred.

Can you help with UCAT preparation for a student at Norwich School applying to medicine?

Yes. UCAT preparation is one of our specialist areas. We work with students on each UCAT section — Verbal Reasoning, Quantitative Reasoning, Abstract Reasoning, Decision Making, and Situational Judgement — providing strategy, timed practice, and performance analysis in the months leading up to their sitting.
