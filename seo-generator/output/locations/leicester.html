Leicester is a city with real academic ambition. Families across Oadby, Stoneygate, and Knighton are increasingly focused on securing the best possible outcomes for their children — whether that means strong GCSE results, competitive A-Level grades, or a place at a Russell Group university. There is particular demand among Leicester families for support aimed at University of Leicester Medical School entry, where competition is fierce and preparation needs to begin well before application season. Tutoring in Leicester — What We Offer

Leading Tuition provides one-to-one private tuition for students from primary age through to sixth form and beyond. Our tutors are experienced, subject-specialist professionals who understand the specific demands of the schools, syllabuses, and universities that Leicester families are working towards. Sessions are available online, giving families in Wigston, Evington, and across the wider Leicestershire area the same quality of support without the need for travel.

We work with students attending a wide range of local schools, including Leicester Grammar School, Stoneygate School, and Gartree School, and we tailor our approach to the specific curriculum and expectations each student faces. Whether your child needs help building foundational skills, closing gaps ahead of exams, or pushing for the highest grades, we match them with a tutor who understands exactly what is required.

GCSE and A-Level Support

The majority of secondary schools in Leicester follow AQA and OCR specifications, and our tutors are thoroughly familiar with both. This matters more than it might seem — exam technique, mark scheme language, and the structure of assessments vary significantly between boards, and a tutor who knows the specification inside out can make a genuine difference to a student's grade.

At GCSE, we support students across all core and optional subjects, with particular demand for Maths, English Language, English Literature, the Sciences, and History. At A-Level, our most requested subjects include Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Further Mathematics, and Economics — subjects where the step up from GCSE is steep and where strong tuition can be the deciding factor between a conditional offer and a missed grade.

  • AQA GCSE and A-Level — Maths, English, Sciences, History, and more
  • OCR A-Level — Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics
  • Structured exam technique and past paper practice
  • Support for students retaking or sitting early entry examinations
  • Targeted revision programmes in the lead-up to the summer exam series

For students at Loughborough Grammar School, where academic expectations are high and the pace is demanding, we regularly support pupils who want to consolidate their understanding and ensure they are performing consistently, not just in bursts before assessments.

11+ and Grammar School Preparation

Leicester itself does not operate a grammar school system, so the traditional 11+ examination is not a feature of the local state school landscape in the way it is in some neighbouring counties. However, many families in Oadby and Stoneygate choose independent schools — such as Leicester Grammar School or Stoneygate School — which have their own entrance assessments, and preparation for these requires focused, structured support.

Leading Tuition works with primary-age children and early secondary students to build the reasoning, literacy, and numeracy skills that underpin strong performance in any entrance or assessment process. We also support the transition into Year 7, helping students settle into the demands of secondary school with confidence rather than anxiety.

Medicine and University Admissions Preparation

The University of Leicester Medical School is one of the most respected in the country, and it attracts highly competitive applicants from across the UK. For Leicester students aspiring to study Medicine — whether at Leicester or at other leading institutions — the admissions process requires preparation that goes well beyond strong A-Level grades.

Leading Tuition provides dedicated support for the UCAT (University Clinical Aptitude Test), which is now used by the University of Leicester and the majority of UK medical schools as a core part of their admissions process. We also support students with personal statement development, interview preparation, and the broader academic strengthening needed to make a competitive application to Russell Group universities across all disciplines.

For students aiming at Oxford or Cambridge, we offer subject-specific preparation that addresses both the academic rigour of admissions tests and the style of thinking that interviews demand.

Frequently Asked Questions about Tutoring in Leicester

Does Leading Tuition work with students at Leicester Grammar School specifically?

Yes. We regularly support students at Leicester Grammar School across a range of subjects and year groups. Our tutors are familiar with the pace and expectations of independent school curricula, and we tailor sessions to complement what students are covering in school rather than duplicating it.

My child is in Year 6 in Oadby — is it worth starting tuition even without a grammar school 11+ to prepare for?

Absolutely. Many families in Oadby use this stage to prepare for independent school entrance assessments or simply to ensure their child transitions into secondary school with strong foundations in Maths and English. Early support at this stage often prevents the gaps that become harder to close later.

Which exam boards do your Leicester tutors cover at GCSE and A-Level?

Our tutors cover both AQA and OCR specifications, which are the most commonly used boards across Leicester secondary schools. When we match a tutor to your child, we confirm the exact specification they are studying so that all support is directly relevant to their assessments.

Can Leading Tuition help with UCAT preparation for University of Leicester Medical School entry?

Yes. We offer structured UCAT preparation that covers all five sections of the test, with a focus on both accuracy and the time management skills that the exam demands. We also support the wider application process, including personal statements and interview readiness, for students applying to Leicester and other UK medical schools.
