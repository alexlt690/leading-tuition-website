Bath has one of the most striking concentrations of independent school excellence in the South West, and its families reflect this. King Edward's School Bath, Prior Park College, and Kingswood School are all academically strong schools with competitive entry, and the University of Bath — consistently ranked among the UK's top institutions for engineering, pharmacy, and the sciences — creates strong A-Level aspirations among families across the city. Whether you are in Larkhall, Oldfield Park, or the villages of Bradford-on-Avon, the expectation that education leads somewhere meaningful is a consistent feature of life in Bath. At Leading Tuition, we support families through every stage of this journey.

Tutoring in Bath — What We Offer

Leading Tuition provides one-to-one tuition from primary through to sixth form and university admissions. Our tutors are subject specialists matched to your child's exact school, syllabus, and target outcomes. Whether your child attends King Edward's School Bath, Prior Park College, Kingswood School, Beechen Cliff School, Hayesfield Girls' School, or another local school, we find the right tutor for their needs.

Sessions are available online and in person across Bath, Larkhall, Bathampton, Oldfield Park, Weston, and Bradford-on-Avon.

  • GCSE and A-Level support across all subjects
  • Tutors experienced with OCR specifications dominant in Bath and North East Somerset schools
  • Independent school support for King Edward's, Prior Park, and Kingswood
  • Medicine and university admissions preparation including UCAT
  • Flexible online and in-person sessions

GCSE and A-Level Support

Bath and North East Somerset schools follow predominantly OCR specifications, and our tutors are matched to students based on their exact board and course. Students at King Edward's School Bath are working within one of the South West's most academically demanding independent environments — the pace is high, the cohort is able, and the expectation is A-Level results that open doors to competitive universities.

For students at Beechen Cliff School or Hayesfield Girls' School — both well-regarded state schools — one-to-one support provides the individual attention that allows able students to reach the top of their ability range. At GCSE and A-Level, we support students across all subjects, with particular strength in the sciences, mathematics, English, and humanities.

The University of Bath's reputation in engineering, pharmacy, chemistry, and the sciences creates a local culture of ambition in these subjects — and our tutors in mathematics, physics, chemistry, and biology are among our strongest.

11+ and Independent School Preparation

Bath and North East Somerset has no grammar schools, so secondary transfer here does not involve the local authority 11+. However, King Edward's School Bath and Prior Park College both set their own entrance assessments, and the standard required reflects their academic ambition.

For families considering Prior Park — a Catholic independent with a broad intake and a strong sixth form — or Kingswood School, which has both day and boarding options, the entrance process is competitive and specific. We work with children from Year 3 upwards to build the foundational skills that underpin strong performance in independent school entrance assessments: mathematical fluency, reading comprehension, and written clarity under timed conditions.

Medicine and University Admissions Preparation

Bath's proximity to Bristol — and the shared aspiration that both cities generate — means that significant numbers of local sixth-formers apply to medicine, pharmacy, engineering, and other highly competitive programmes. Leading Tuition supports these students with UCAT preparation, personal statement development, MMI interview coaching, and admissions strategy. For students targeting the University of Bath's highly competitive pharmacy and pharmacology programmes, or aiming at medical schools in Bristol, Exeter, or London, specialist preparation is a practical advantage.

Why Bath Families Choose Leading Tuition

Bath families are discerning and well-informed. They understand that the quality of a tutor matters, not just the number of sessions. Our tutors are Oxford and Cambridge graduates selected for their subject expertise and their ability to communicate clearly with young people. We match carefully, communicate honestly, and take seriously the responsibility of supporting a child during a high-stakes period of their education.

Frequently Asked Questions about Tutoring in Bath

How competitive is entry to King Edward's School Bath?

King Edward's is academically selective and sets its own entrance assessments in Mathematics and English. It is one of the South West's most respected day schools, and competition for places reflects this. Children who are well prepared — with strong foundational skills and familiarity with the assessment format — approach the process with genuine confidence.

My daughter is at Hayesfield Girls' School and working towards A-Level Biology for a medicine application — can you help?

Yes. Biology is one of the subjects where we have particular strength, and A-Level Biology for medicine applicants requires a depth of understanding that goes beyond what is strictly needed to pass the exam. We match students with tutors who know the OCR specification thoroughly and can support both examination performance and the genuine subject understanding that medical school interviews test.

We are in Bradford-on-Avon — do you cover our area?

Yes. We work with families across Bath, Larkhall, Bathampton, Oldfield Park, Weston, and Bradford-on-Avon. Most sessions are conducted online, but in-person is available where preferred.

Can you support a student applying to the University of Bath for pharmacy?

Yes. Bath's pharmacy programme is highly competitive, and the personal statement for this course needs to demonstrate genuine engagement with the subject — not generic enthusiasm. We support students with personal statement development, interview preparation, and the A-Level Chemistry and Biology support that underpins a strong pharmacy application.
