Private Tuition in Bath

Expert tutors supporting families across Bath

Bath is a city with exceptionally high academic expectations. The presence of a thriving independent school sector — including King Edward's School Bath, Prior Park College, and Kingswood School — alongside the University of Bath and easy access to Bristol's universities means that families here are thinking seriously about education from an early age. Competition for places at the leading independent schools is real, A-Level results matter enormously, and parents in areas like Larkhall, Weston, and Oldfield Park are increasingly turning to private tuition to give their children the focused support that busy classrooms simply cannot always provide. Leading Tuition works with Bath families to meet those pressures with structured, expert teaching — delivered online or in person, and tailored to each child's specific needs.

Tutoring in Bath — What We Offer

Leading Tuition provides one-to-one tutoring for students from primary age through to A-Level and university admissions preparation. Our tutors are subject specialists, not generalists, and every student is matched carefully based on their year group, school, exam board, and learning style. Whether your child is preparing for entrance assessments at one of Bath's independent schools, working through a difficult GCSE module, or building confidence ahead of A-Level examinations, we offer consistent, high-quality support that fits around family life.

Sessions are available online or face-to-face, and we work with families across Bath and the surrounding area, including Bradford-on-Avon and Bathampton. For parents who want flexibility without sacrificing quality, our online sessions use the same structured approach as in-person teaching — with shared resources, recorded notes, and regular progress updates so you always know how your child is getting on.

  • Primary and early secondary support — building strong foundations in English and Maths before the demands of GCSE begin
  • GCSE tuition — covering OCR, AQA, and Edexcel specifications across core and optional subjects
  • A-Level tuition — specialist subject teaching aligned to the exact syllabus your child is studying
  • Independent school entrance preparation — tailored to the specific assessments used by Bath's leading schools
  • University admissions support — including UCAT preparation and personal statement coaching

GCSE and A-Level Support

Bath's secondary schools are academically ambitious. Students at Beechen Cliff School and Hayesfield Girls' School, as well as those in the independent sector, are frequently working towards OCR qualifications — the dominant exam board across many Bath schools — though AQA and Edexcel specifications are also well represented. Our tutors are familiar with all three boards and know exactly where the marks are won and lost at each level.

At GCSE, the jump in demand and complexity catches many students off guard, particularly in the sciences, Mathematics, and English Literature. Our tutors work through past papers, mark schemes, and examiner reports so that students understand not just the content but the specific way OCR and other boards expect answers to be structured. At A-Level, where independent study becomes far more important, we help students develop the analytical depth and exam technique that top grades require. This is especially relevant for students at King Edward's School Bath and Prior Park College, where the academic bar is high and university destinations are competitive.

11+ and Grammar School Preparation

Bath itself does not have grammar schools, so the 11+ is not the defining milestone for local families that it is in some other parts of England. However, many parents in Bath are focused on entrance assessments for independent schools — Prior Park College, Kingswood School, and King Edward's School Bath all have their own admissions processes, and preparation for these requires a different kind of support to the standard curriculum.

Leading Tuition also works with primary-age children and those in early secondary school who would benefit from consolidating core skills in Maths and English. Building strong foundations at Year 4, 5, or 6 makes a significant difference to how confidently a child approaches the transition to secondary school, whether that is a state school or an independent one. We treat this stage seriously — it is not about drilling children, but about developing genuine understanding and the kind of quiet confidence that carries them forward.

Medicine and University Admissions Preparation

With the University of Bath on the doorstep and Bristol's medical and dental schools within easy reach, a notable number of Bath sixth-formers are applying to highly competitive university courses. Medicine, dentistry, veterinary science, and law all require preparation that goes well beyond strong A-Level grades.

Leading Tuition supports students through UCAT preparation — the admissions test now used by Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial, and the majority of UK medical schools — as well as LNAT coaching for law applicants. We also offer personal statement support, helping students articulate their motivations clearly and honestly without falling into the clichés that admissions tutors see repeatedly. For students at A-Level who are targeting Russell Group or Oxbridge offers, we can also provide subject-specific extension work to deepen understanding beyond the syllabus.

Why Bath Families Choose Leading Tuition

Parents in Bath are not simply looking for someone to sit with their child and go over homework. They want a tutor who understands the specific schools their child attends, the exam boards those schools use, and the academic culture of the city. Leading Tuition brings that local knowledge together with rigorous subject expertise and a genuine commitment to each student's progress.

We are straightforward about what tuition can and cannot do. We do not promise overnight transformations. What we do offer is consistent, well-planned teaching from tutors who care about the students they work with — and a clear process for tracking progress so that parents feel informed rather than anxious. Families in Weston, Larkhall, Bradford-on-Avon, and across Bath have found that this approach makes a real difference, not just to grades but to how their children feel about learning.

Frequently Asked Questions about Tutoring in Bath

My child attends Prior Park College and is struggling with A-Level Chemistry — can you help with the OCR specification specifically?

Yes. Our science tutors are familiar with the OCR A-Level Chemistry specification and work through it in detail, including the practical endorsement requirements. We can identify exactly where your child is losing marks and build a structured plan around their school's teaching schedule.

Are there tutors available in areas like Bathampton or Bradford-on-Avon, or is everything based in central Bath?

We work with families across the wider Bath area, including Bathampton and Bradford-on-Avon. In-person sessions can often be arranged depending on tutor availability, and our online sessions are equally effective — many families in outlying areas find online tuition more practical without any loss of quality.

My daughter is in Year 6 at a Bath primary school. Is it too early to start tuition if she is not sitting an 11+?

Not at all. Year 6 is an excellent time to consolidate Maths and English skills ahead of secondary school. Even without a selective admissions test to prepare for, students who arrive at secondary school with strong foundations settle in more quickly and find the step up in expectations far less daunting.

How does Leading Tuition keep parents informed about progress?

After each session, parents receive a brief update covering what was covered, how the student performed, and what will be focused on next. We also carry out more detailed progress reviews at regular intervals, so you have a clear picture of where your child stands — not just reassurance that sessions are happening.
