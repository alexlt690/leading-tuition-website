Twickenham and the wider Royal Borough of Richmond upon Thames combine two of the most educationally distinct pressures in South West London: proximity to Tiffin School and Tiffin Girls' School in neighbouring Kingston, and a strong independent school sector led by Hampton School and Lady Eleanor Holles School. Families in Richmond, St Margarets, Hampton, and Teddington are making decisions at 11+ that carry very significant weight, and many are navigating both the grammar school and independent school routes simultaneously. At Leading Tuition, we support families through both.

Tutoring in Twickenham — What We Offer

Leading Tuition provides one-to-one tuition from primary through to sixth form and university admissions. Our tutors are subject specialists matched to your child's exact school, syllabus, and target outcomes. Whether your child attends Hampton School, Lady Eleanor Holles School, Orleans Park School, Waldegrave School for Girls, Radnor House School, or is preparing for entry to Tiffin, we find the right tutor for their needs.

GCSE and A-Level Support

Hampton School and Lady Eleanor Holles School are two of South West London's most academically selective independent schools, with outstanding A-Level results and strong Oxbridge track records. Students at these schools are working within demanding environments, and one-to-one support that is targeted, board-specific, and taught by someone who has been through a comparably rigorous academic experience makes a real difference.

The Royal Borough's schools follow predominantly AQA and OCR specifications. At GCSE and A-Level, we support students across all subjects, with particular strength in mathematics, further mathematics, sciences, economics, and English — the subjects where demand from Twickenham and Richmond families is most consistent.

11+ and Grammar School Preparation

The competition for places at Tiffin School and Tiffin Girls' School is intense, and families in Richmond upon Thames are among those who travel for these places in significant numbers. The assessment tests verbal reasoning, non-verbal reasoning, English comprehension, and mathematics — and the standard required is very high.

Alongside the Tiffin grammar schools, Hampton School at 11+ and Lady Eleanor Holles School both have their own selective entry processes with a different character from the local authority grammar format. Radnor House School in Twickenham itself also sets an entrance assessment. Families often find themselves preparing for multiple routes simultaneously, and we can advise on how to do this efficiently.

We begin 11+ and entrance preparation with a thorough assessment of where your child currently stands, then build a structured programme that develops the skills being tested across all routes, ensuring nothing is left to chance on the day.

Medicine and University Admissions Preparation

Hampton School and Lady Eleanor Holles regularly send students to Oxbridge and Russell Group medicine programmes. Leading Tuition supports these students with UCAT preparation, Oxbridge admissions test coaching (MAT, LNAT, TSA, and others), personal statement development, and interview preparation. For students targeting the most competitive courses, specialist admissions preparation is an essential complement to strong A-Level grades.

Our tutors are Oxford and Cambridge graduates who understand the specific schools, examinations, and admissions processes that Twickenham families care about. Frequently Asked Questions about Tutoring in Twickenham

Should we target Tiffin or Hampton School for our son at 11+?

Both are excellent schools, and many families target both simultaneously. The assessments are different in format and emphasis: the Tiffin assessment is the local authority grammar test, while Hampton sets its own entrance papers with a different character. With structured preparation, targeting both is achievable — and the skills that help a child perform well in one often support performance in the other. We can advise on a preparation strategy that covers both efficiently.

My daughter is at Lady Eleanor Holles and working towards Oxbridge — can you help?

Yes. Oxbridge preparation — including admissions test coaching, personal statement development, and interview preparation — is one of our specialist areas. We work with students at LEH and other academically selective schools on the specific preparation their target course requires, going well beyond what schools can usually offer in a group context.

We are in Richmond — do you cover our area?

Yes. We work with families across Twickenham, Richmond, St Margarets, Hampton, Whitton, and Teddington. Most sessions are conducted online, but in-person is available where preferred.

Can you support UCAT preparation for a student at Hampton Sixth Form applying to medicine?

Yes. UCAT preparation is one of our specialist areas. We work with students on section-by-section strategy, timed practice, and performance analysis in the months leading up to their sitting in the summer of Year 12/13. Hampton students are typically very able, but the UCAT requires specific skills — speed, decision-making under pressure, and abstract reasoning — that are different from A-Level academic skills and need targeted preparation.
