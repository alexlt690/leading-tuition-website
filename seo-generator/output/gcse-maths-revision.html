GCSE Maths Revision

Here's a parent-facing paragraph for a UK tutoring website: --- **Supporting Your Child Through GCSE Maths Revision** GCSE Maths can feel overwhelming for many students, but with the right structure, real progress is absolutely achievable. The current specification — whether your child is sitting AQA, Edexcel, or OCR — covers key topics including algebra, ratio and proportion, geometry, probability, and statistics, all assessed across three papers (one non-calculator and two calculator). Encouraging your child to practise past papers regularly, ideally under timed conditions, is one of the most effective revision strategies, as it builds both familiarity with question styles and confidence under pressure. Our tutors work closely with each student to identify specific gaps in their understanding, rather than simply revisiting everything from scratch, so revision sessions are focused and time-efficient. Whether your child is targeting a Grade 4 pass or pushing for a Grade 7 and above, tailored one-to-one support can make a significant difference in the months leading up to their exams.
