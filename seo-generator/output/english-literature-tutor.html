## English Literature Tutoring Our English Literature tutors help students at every level — from Key Stage 3 through to A Level — to read more confidently, write more analytically, and engage meaningfully with the texts on their syllabus. Whether your child is struggling to structure an essay, unsure how to approach unseen poetry, or preparing for GCSE and A Level exams with AQA, Edexcel, or OCR, our tutors provide targeted, personalised support tailored to their specific board and set texts. Sessions focus on building the core skills that examiners reward: close textual analysis, use of literary terminology, and the ability to construct a well-evidenced argument. Beyond exam technique, we encourage a genuine love of literature — helping students find their own voice and develop the critical thinking skills that will serve them well beyond school.
