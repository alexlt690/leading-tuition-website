## Choosing the Right GCSE Maths Tutor for Your Child Finding the right GCSE Maths tutor can make a genuine difference to your child's confidence and results. A good tutor will work through the full AQA, Edexcel, or OCR specification with your child, identifying specific gaps in topics such as algebra, ratio, probability, and geometry, rather than simply repeating what is covered in school. Sessions are typically tailored to your child's current grade and target grade, with regular practice using past papers to build exam technique and time management skills. Whether your child is aiming to secure a grade 4 pass or pushing for a grade 7, 8, or 9, a qualified and experienced GCSE Maths tutor provides structured, one-to-one support that a busy classroom simply cannot offer. Most tutors offer flexible online or in-person sessions, making it straightforward to fit tuition around school, homework, and family life.
